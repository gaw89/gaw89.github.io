There's a Common Theme...

Managing clinical resources is hard!

Problem:

Managing the Technician Schedule is Time Consuming and Painful

10+ Hours per Week!

Why?

  • Human Factors
    • Vacation
    • Callouts
    • Location/Physician Preferences
    • Time Preferences
    • Training Differences

  • Large Number of Personnel
    • 50 Clinical Faculty
    • 60+ Technicians
    • 8 Photographers
    • 16 Scribes

Solution:

Increase availability of information for tech managers
  • Personnel Information
    • Where are they?
    • When are they there?
    • What specific skills do they have?
    • Who do they work best with?

  • Clinical Needs
    • How many techs does each doc need?
    • What skillset does each clinic require?
    • What time are the techs needed?

  • Optimize Decision Process

Problem:

Managing the Physician Schedule & Opening New Sessions is Challenging

Why?

  • Information is not available to those who need it
    • Room Availability
    • Technician Availability
    • Scribe Availability

  • Even when we know, we don't know far enough in advance to do anything about it

  • There is no standardized process for opening rooms
    • Send an email?
    • Ask your AA to send an email?
    • Who do you email?
    • Talk to tech manager in the hallway?
    • Send your favorite carrier pigeon?

Solution:

  • Track room availability
  • Track personnel availability
  • Create standardized process for opening sessions

Problem:

Managing Room Utilization is Difficult

Room Utilization is < 80%!

Why?

  • Many Road Blocks
    • We don't know when there are rooms available
    • There's no standard mechanism to sign out available rooms
    • Can't Do Attitude
      • We don't have enough techs
      • There aren't any rooms available
      • I'm not sure right now, I'll let you know when we get closer to the date
